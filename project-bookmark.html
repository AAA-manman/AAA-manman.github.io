Bookmark made at Millersville University

For this Print Project, we were tasked to design a bookmark for elementary school kids at Eshleman Elementary School. I was aiming to stand out to a more niche audience of kids, and to an extent, myself. I often struggle with feeling inadequate, and maybe my bookmark can help at least a tenth of a kid to think they are capable of great things.

We started out by sketching 5 different bookmark ideas and continued with 2 of them. At least everyone else continued with 2 of theirs. I was more absorbed with my one bookmark idea, and I went all in with that one.

I scoured all over the internet for clipart to use in the bookmark, and I spent even more time contructing the 2 horizon lines (68723695+ years) but I got this project finished on time.

There was a whole process on turning digital files into real bookmarks that I don't understand at all, so I'm not going to talk about it.

I'm proud of how my bookmark came out though,
